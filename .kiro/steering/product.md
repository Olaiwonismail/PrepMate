# Product Overview

PrepMate is an AI-powered mock interview platform that helps candidates practice technical and behavioral interviews through realistic voice-driven conversations.

## Core Value Proposition

Practice interviews until they feel easy by engaging with an AI interviewer that asks real questions, listens to answers, and generates contextual follow-ups — simulating real interview dynamics.

## Key Features

- **AI Question Generation**: Generates 5 tailored interview questions from job descriptions using Google Gemini
- **Voice Interaction**: Natural voice conversations powered by ElevenLabs TTS/STT
- **Dynamic Follow-ups**: AI generates contextual follow-up questions based on candidate responses
- **Detailed Debrief**: Comprehensive feedback with scores (0-10), strengths, and improvement areas for each question
- **Interview Types**: Supports behavioral, technical, or mixed interview formats

## Interview Structure

Every interview follows a 5-question format:

1. **The Icebreaker**: Relevant project experience
2. **The Core Concept**: Technical knowledge deep dive
3. **The System Design**: Architecture and design thinking
4. **The Behavioral**: Soft skills and team dynamics (STAR format)
5. **The Production Fire**: Crisis response and pressure handling

## User Flow

1. User pastes job description and selects interview type
2. AI generates 5 tailored questions
3. User answers via voice or text
4. AI generates follow-up questions based on responses
5. User receives detailed debrief with scores and feedback
